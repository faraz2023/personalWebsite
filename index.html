The artful artifact? work of art? the art? I can't see none as such! What I find excavating my memories are experiences of artifacts, artful in the moment of encounter. Art? It is probably just an experience. A disturbing pause! An inarticulate pin point in the history of my madness. Art? The artful experience. The tragic experience of standing still amid all the arbitrary violence and suffering of a comical world. Isn't this problematic? It certainty is! It is not enough to seek art anymore. Now one need to ask itself; how is it possible to have an artful experience? A being, stretched in time, needs to ask itself; sunk in it, how should I freeze this flux and bring it before my eyes? One becomes the history itself, witnessing the artifact. The point is to watch the obvious all over again and getting brutally interrogated by it!
